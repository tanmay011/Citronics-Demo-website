Blind Coding


Description

Welcome to the ultimate challenge of Coding. Are you confident enough to code simple programs without looking at your monitor screen? Then You Are at the Right Place!
Blind coding is a C/C++ programming event where competitors need to write the program when the monitors are switched off. Beware! You can't see the bugs that you have produced.

Eligibility Criteria: Anyone with a knack for Programming.
Programming Language: C / C++
. Compiler: Turbo C++ on Windows/G++ on linux.

REGISTRATION FEE: Rs100/-

Guidelines

1. This is an individual event. Only 1 participant shall be allowed in a single registration.
2. Participant shall be provided with one PC (without mouse).
3. Participant shall be provided with a problem statement only.
4. Monitor shall remain powered off / covered by black cloth piece while the participant is coding.
5. Participant is allowed to type his / her code till the allowed time limit only.
6. The level of difficulty increases as you level up.
7. No. of levels and difficulty shall depend upon the number of registrations for the event.
